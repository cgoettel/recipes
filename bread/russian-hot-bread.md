# Russian hot bread

## Ingredients
- 2&frac12; t yeast
- &frac14; C sugar
- 1 C warm water
- 1 egg
- &frac34; stick butter
- 1 T salt

## Directions
- Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit.
- Separately, mix egg, butter, and salt. Slowly add 3 C flour.
- Let rise.
- Bake appropriately for use, generally hot (450&deg;F).

Works great as pizza dough.
