1/4 C shortening
1/4 C sugar
1 1/2 t salt
3/4 C scalded milk
1 T yeast
3/4 C water
pinch sugar
1 egg
3-4 C flour

dissolve sugar, salt, and shortening into scalded milk. cool

dissolve yeast, warm water, pinch of sugar in seperate bowl

combine

add in beaten egg. add 2 C flour. beat. add flour. knead

dough should be a little sticky. let rise. divide in 3 sections. braid. let rise. cook.

375F for 30-35 min. beat egg w/ water. put on top.
