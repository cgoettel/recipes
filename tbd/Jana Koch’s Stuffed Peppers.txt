6 medium peppers  (remove tops, seeds and membranes

Drop peppers in boiling salt water for 5 mins.  Set in casserole dish.

In skillet, lightly brown:
1 lb ground beef
½ C chopped onions
½ tsp salt
Dash of pepper
 
Then add:
1, 1 lbs can stewed tomatoes
1/2 C water
1/2 c uncooked long grain rice
1 tsp wors. Sauce


Simmer until the rice is tender about 15 min and moisture is mostly cooked down. Then stir in 1 C. grated cheese. Stuff peppers and sprinkle with bread crumbs and parmesan cheese.

Bake uncovered 350  for 20-25 minutes.
