##Ingredients

- 6-9 cloves garlic
- 3-4 Roma tomatoes
- 3 T salt
- 1-2 C white vinegar
- &frac14; C powdered chicken broth
- 1-2 t paprika

##Directions

- Add all ingredients (except crushed tomatoes) to blender. Blend.
- Add tomatoes to color (and mild).
