Ingredients
    * 4.5C of flour for bread Francine
    * 2.5C g of water
    * 1.5 tsp. teaspoon of salt
    * 2 tsp. dry baker's yeast

Mix salt and flour

Pour yeast in the fountain of flour

Add warm water

Mix with fingertips gradually incorporating the flour

Form the dough into a ball

Knead the dough vigorously for 6 minutes

Place dough in a bowl

Cover with a cloth and let rise in a warm place

30 minutes (minimum) later
Place the dough on the floured work surface

Flatten the doughto form a square

Fold the sides of the square of dough to form a coil

Train ends into points

Put on pan

Let rise about forty minutes in a warm place

Score the surface with a baker's blade

Bake in preheated oven at 464°F and reduce the temperature to 392°F.
Bake until crust is golden brown.
